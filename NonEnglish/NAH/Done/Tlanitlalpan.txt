Tlanitlalpan

In Hueyitlahtohcayotl in Tlanitlalpan (Tlanitlalpantlahtolli: Nederland ahnozo Koninkrijk der Nederlanden) tlacatiyan ipan Europan. Tlanitlalpan ineixcahuiltoca ca, ahmo melahuac itoca Holanda. Amsterdam in achcauhtecuacan ca, In Haag in tetlamahmacani tecuacan ca.

In Tlanitlalpan tlatectli ipan in mictlancihuatlampa Europan, cuaxochtia in mictlampa ica in Mictlampa Hueyatl, in huitztlampa ica Belgica ihuan in tlapcopa ica Alemania. In tlacatiyan chihua ce tlalli huel pozahuacayoca chantililli ihuan ce huel neicnelililli: ipal in 2008 ocatca tlatectli ipan in inic chiucnahui itechpa in tlacatl ineicneliliz ic in Tlacetililli Tlacatiyan.
